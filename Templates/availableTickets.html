Jeopardy! Priority Seating Request Form

Attend a taping of the show - Live!

This is the official Jeopardy! tickets request page. Tickets to see a taping of Jeopardy! are free, but they are also extremely limited. Your tickets allow you to be among the first group of people to enter the TV studio on the day of taping, and are available on a first come, first served basis only. Just fill out the form below to reserve seats for up to four members per party. Available show times for the current season are posted below and will be updated regularly. If no convenient show times are listed in the drop-down menu, please check back at a later time.

Ticket Details

  • All fields are required.
  • No purchase necessary.
  • The following page will serve as your confirmation for the receipt of ticket(s). Print and save.
  • All expenses incurred are the responsibility of the participant.
  • All dates and times are subject to change at the Producer's sole discretion. The dates above are currently the only dates available. Please bookmark this page and check back each month for new tapings if your preferred date is not listed above. It is anticipated that demand will exceed supply.
  • Please bring a valid government-issued photo ID upon arrival. This information must match the information submitted with your request / confirmation. By entering the SPE building you will also be required to undergo a metal detector screening and a bag check. Please refrain from bringing extra personal belongings. Large bags and backpacks are prohibited.
  • By entering the SPE building you hereby consent to video surveillance and a search of your vehicle and/or personal belongings at any time.
  • Due to production requirements, children under age 8 will not be admitted.
  • Smoking, food, beverages, recording equipment, and weapons of any kind are strictly prohibited.
  • SPE strictly prohibits the unauthorized use of cameras or any other recording devices - including cell phone cameras. Use of any recording device must be approved by/or at the direction of the Audience Coordinators. Cell phones must be turned off during tapings.
  • Only the requests made through this page will receive priority seating. Your priority seating ticket request takes precedence over any other ticketing outlet.
  • For Individual tickets and groups up to four persons, you MUST confirm your seats within 5 - 7 days of your tape date. If you do not confirm your seats within this time frame your seats are subject to cancellation. To confirm your seats please call 818-773-3465.
